# Troubleshooting Bots/Apps

If your Bot or App isn’t working properly, don’t worry — try the following steps:

### **1. Check Permissions**

* Make sure the Bot/App has been granted the necessary permissions to operate in your Clan.
* Refer to [**Manage Bots & Apps**](manage-bots-and-apps.md) for detailed instructions.

### **2. Remove and Re-Invite**

* Sometimes, simply removing and re-inviting the Bot/App can resolve temporary issues.

### **3. Check for Updates**

* Developers regularly release updates to fix bugs and enhance performance.
* Visit [**top.mezon.ai**](https://top.mezon.ai) or the Bot/App’s support channel to see if a new version is available.

### **4. Contact Mezon Support**

If the above steps don’t solve the problem, don’t hesitate to reach out to our Support Team — we’re here to help 24/7!
